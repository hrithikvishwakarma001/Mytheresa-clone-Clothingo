One of the fashion industry’s most-revered names, Givenchy creates iconic looks with a twist. The house’s roots in couture are exemplified today in the form of luxurious eveningwear and modern tailoring.

Hubert de Givenchy founded his eponymous house in 1952. Dubbed the “enfant terrible” of haute couture, the designer evoked a new mood in the ‘50s with his refreshing point of view – defined by clean, architectural silhouettes and a touch of romanticism. The brand is now under the creative direction of Matthew M. Williams.
